The Best Movies According to Hasan

My to 3 movies of all-time

Peaky Blinders

Peaky Blinders is a British crime drama television series set in Birmingham, England, that follows the exploits of the Shelby crime family in the aftermath of World War I.
The show is known for its stylish cinematography, complex characters, and gripping storylines.

All of Us Are Dead

All of Us Are Dead is a South Korean zombie apocalypse television series based on the webtoon "Now at Our School."
It follows a group of high school students trapped in their school during a zombie outbreak, fighting for survival while dealing with personal conflicts and the horrors of the situation.
The series has been praised for its intense action, character development, and social commentary.

Crash landing on you

Crash Landing on You is a South Korean romantic drama television series that tells the story of a South Korean heiress who accidentally paraglides into North Korea and meets a North Korean officer.
The series explores their forbidden love amidst the backdrop of political tensions and cultural differences, blending romance, comedy, and drama.
It has received acclaim for its engaging plot, chemistry between the leads, and portrayal of inter-Korean relations.
